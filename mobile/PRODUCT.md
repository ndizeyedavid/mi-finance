# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are individuals tracking personal finances—managing spending, budgets, and savings in a calm, focused environment. Users are from Rwanda and the currency to be used is RWF.

## Product Purpose

A clean, elegant personal finance tracker that helps users understand and manage their money without overwhelming them. Success means users can quickly see their financial picture and make confident decisions.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, elegant, sophisticated. Calm and trustworthy, with a focus on clarity and simplicity. Uses claymorphism design for a soft, tactile feel.

## Brand Colors

- Primary: #58a49f
- Success: #25A969
- Danger: #F95B51

## Anti-references

- Avoid busy/overwhelming UI
- Avoid cartoonish/playful aesthetics
- Keep it professional and focused

## Design Principles

1. Clarity over decoration—every element serves a purpose
2. Soft tactility—use claymorphism to feel approachable
3. Confidence through consistency—predictable patterns build trust
4. Accessibility first—no user left behind

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Color blindness considerations (use patterns + color)
- Support for reduced motion
- Clear, readable typography
